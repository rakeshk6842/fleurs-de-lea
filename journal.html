Flower care

How do I make my flowers last longer?

Add fresh, cool water daily, change the water fully every 1–2 days, trim stems at an angle, and keep arrangements away from direct sun and heat sources.

What if a flower starts to wilt?

Remove any spent blooms, refresh the vase water, and give the stems a fresh trim. If an issue appears unusually early, contact the studio and they can help troubleshoot.

Do you include care instructions?

Yes. Each arrangement comes with simple guidance so you can enjoy the flowers for as long as possible once they arrive.
