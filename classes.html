What each class includes

Everything you need for a polished, hands-on floral experience

  • 2 hours of guided, hands-on instruction
  • Premium fresh floral varieties and greenery
  • Vase and/or floral foam, wire, and tape
  • Packaging box to transport your flowers home
  • Personalized attention with a maximum of 6 students
  • Instruction shaped around different learning styles and comfort levels

Please bring a sharp cutting tool, or preorder professional floral shears when registering. Free onsite parking is available at the Fairfax City studio.

Design classes include: bouquets, centerpieces, wreaths, floral jewelry, corsages, boutonnieres, hairpieces, bridal bouquets, arch décor, and more.
