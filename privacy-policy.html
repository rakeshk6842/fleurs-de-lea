Privacy & refund information

Clear expectations help every order feel smooth, personal, and well cared for.

Privacy

Contact details submitted through the inquiry forms are used only to respond to requests, coordinate orders, and provide service updates.

Substitutions

Seasonal flowers vary. If a stem is unavailable, it may be replaced with a design-forward alternative of similar value and style.

Timing

Please share delivery windows, venue notes, and special requests as early as possible so the studio can plan carefully.

Refunds & order changes

Custom floral work is planned around stem availability and design time. Because of that, same-day cancellations may not be refundable once work has begun.

For event, wedding, and custom orders, any payment schedule or cancellation terms shared in your quote or invoice will govern that order.

Questions?

If you need help with an order, delivery issue, pickup timing, or a design concern, reach out directly and the studio will review the request promptly.
